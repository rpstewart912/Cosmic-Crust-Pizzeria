Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Cosmic Crust Pizzeria collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and address when you create an account or place an order.
  • Order Information: Details about the pizzas and other items you order.
  • Usage Information: Information about how you use our website, such as the pages you visit and the actions you take.
  • Device Information: Information about the device you use to access our website, such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for purposes such as:

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders.
  • Communicating with you about your orders and our services.
  • Personalizing your experience on our website.
  • Improving our website and services.
  • Marketing and advertising our products and services (with your consent).

Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our website and provide our services (e.g., payment processors, delivery companies).
  • Marketing and advertising partners (with your consent).
  • Legal authorities when required by law.

Your Choices

You may have the following choices regarding your information:

  • You can access and update your personal information in your account settings.
  • You can opt out of receiving marketing emails from us.
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website.
